Veteran Housing Overview

Cujo’s Neighborhood Rise / Veterans is a purpose-built supportive housing community designed to provide durable, cost-effective, and stable housing solutions for veterans in the High Desert.

The Need

Veterans in the High Desert continue to face housing instability while construction timelines and per-unit costs remain barriers to rapid response.

The Approach

  • Steel-framed, fire-resistant housing units
  • Organized community layout with defined shared spaces
  • Tiered placement to accommodate natural terrain
  • Solar-supported infrastructure
  • On-site service integration
Images displayed are conceptual examples only. Final architectural designs and layouts will reflect site engineering requirements and Cujo Standard specifications.
