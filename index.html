Executive Summary

Cujo’s Neighborhood Rise is a community-based development initiative focused on building permanent and transitional infrastructure rooted in stability, service, and sustainability. Through housing, renewable energy systems, workforce pathways, and integrated commerce, the organization advances scalable models designed for long-term independence and measurable impact. This is structured, durable rise.
